## Hello!

HackTricks is an educational wiki about **cybersecurity** led by Carlos Polop, with hundreds of collaborators. It's a **huge collection of hacking tricks** updated by the community. If you find something missing or outdated, send a **Pull Request** to the [HackTricks GitHub](https://github.com/carlospolop/hacktricks).

HackTricks is also a wiki where **researchers share their latest findings**, making it a great place to stay current with modern attack techniques.

<Note>
Credits for techniques from other researchers belong to the original authors. Kudos to everyone who shares knowledge to improve internet security.
</Note>

## How to Follow and Contribute

- **Track updates** and changelogs on the [PEASS-ng releases](https://github.com/peass-ng/PEASS-ng/releases) page.
- **Report new tricks** or send PRs to keep HackTricks current at [github.com/carlospolop/hacktricks](https://github.com/carlospolop/hacktricks).
- **Structured learning** with the author: check the latest calendar at [hacktricks-training.com](https://hacktricks-training.com/).
